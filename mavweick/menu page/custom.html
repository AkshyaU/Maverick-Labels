Parking Permit Styles

HANG TAGS

Hang tag permits are easily transferable from one vehicle to another and their durable construction allows them to stand up well to extreme temperatures that may occur inside vehicles. We offer the most popular 2.75” x 4.75” size directly on our site, or you can chat with CustomerCare if you're looking for something larger or smaller.

WINDOW STICKERS

Available in the most popular 3” square parking sticker size, these clear stickers come in either clear polyester with face adhesive (removable) or a clear static cling material. Your layout is backed with a layer of white to improve opacity and improve visibility through the window it's applied to.
